How to Remove a Group from a Folder!

Click on the Gear Icon named "settings".

Click on the Link Named "Site contents".

Click on the "Documents" folder.

Click on any subfolder so as to highlight it.

Note: We need to highlight it so as to make the three horizontal dot menu visable.

Click on the three horizontal dots.

Click on the "Manage access" menu option.

Click on the "Groups" button.

Click on any group that doesn't say "Owner".

Click on the Gear Icon named "settings".

Click on the "Can View" Button.

Note: The Button "Can't Download" or "Can Edit" depending on the permission level of the group."

Click on the "Remove direct access" menu option.

Click "Apply".
