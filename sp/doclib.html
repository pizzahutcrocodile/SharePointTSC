Navigating Document Libraries!

Documents Folder!

Click on the Gear Icon named "settings".

Click on the Link Named "Site contents".

Click on the three vertical dot menu next to the folder named "Documents".

Click on the "Settings" menu option.

Click on the link named "Permissions for this document library".

Note: The "Document Folder" Inherits it's groups that exist in "Site Permission".
Meaning all Groups that do exist are automatically added to this Folder.

Note: Observe the limited options in the Permissions Ribbion.
"Manage Parent" referse to "Site permissions" page.
"Stop Inheriting Permissions" means to not inherit from "Site Permissions".

Other Folders!

Click on the Gear Icon named "settings".

Click on the Gear Icon named "settings".

Click on the three vertical dot menu next to the folder named "Site Pages".

Note: This can be done with any other folder library except for "Document Folder". "Site Pages is just being used as an example."

Click on the "Settings" menu option.

Click on the link named "Permissions for this document library".

Note: Unlike the document library permissions options the other libraries gives the option to
"Delete unique permission", "Grant Permissions", "Edit User Permissions", "Remove User Permissiosn".
