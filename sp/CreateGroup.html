How to Create a Group!

Click on the Gear Icon named "settings".

Click on the Link Named "Site permissions".

Click on the Link named "Advanced permissions settings".

Click on the button named "Create Group".

Click on the text box and name the Group.

Note: Remember to name the group as descriptively as possible.

Click on the text box and type the user that will own the Group.

Note: Remember the owner will be to add users in to the group. If more than one person is necessary to own a
group a secondary group has to be made and then that group can be named the owner.

Click and add a Permission Level

Click on the Create Button.
