How to Check Permission Levels!

Click on the Gear Icon named "settings".

Click on the Link Named "Site permissions".

Click on the Link named "Advanced permissions settings".

Click on the "Permission Levels" button.

Note: This is the list of Permisssion Levels, feel free to open each up and read through the specific permissions each
have to familiarize yourself. Be sure not to change of any them since it is best practices not to.

Add Permission Level!

Click on the "Add a Permission Level" button.

Note: Be sure to avoid the "Delete Seleected Permission Levels" button.

Click in the text box. Name your Permission Level and then press enter.

Note: Make sure the name is descriptive of what the permission level is for.

Read through the permissions and click on the ones most appropriate for the permission level
that you have in mind.

When done adding permissions click create.
